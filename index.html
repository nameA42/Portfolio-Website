About

I'm a PhD student in Computational Media at UC Santa Cruz, where I also earned my BS in Computer Game Design (GPA 3.91). I currently TA for CMPM 120 and CMPM 147, and work as a Graduate Student Researcher at the Assistive Design Lab (ADL).

My games span Phaser, Godot, Unity, and Unreal — from browser-playable arcade titles to narrative horror experiences. I'm drawn to rapid prototyping, procedural systems, and audio-driven design. Outside of games, I've competed nationally in Ultimate Frisbee and have a background in fabrication, 3D art, and robotics.
