About

I'm a PhD student in Computational Media at UC Santa Cruz, where I also earned my BS in Computer Game Design (GPA 3.91). I have TA'd for CMPM 120 and CMPM 147, and work with the Assistive Design Lab (ADL).

My research focuses on LLMs and game AI. I'm 2nd author on a Best Paper Nominee at AIIDE 2025 on LLM rule understanding, and a paper under review at IEEE Transactions on Games on card synergy analysis using LLMs.

My games span Phaser, Godot, Unity, and Unreal — from browser-playable arcade titles to narrative horror experiences. I'm drawn to rapid prototyping, procedural systems, and audio-driven design.

I've competed with the UCSC Slugs Ultimate Frisbee team for 5 years, attending nationals twice, and played with Wavestorms in 2025.
